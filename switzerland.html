More About Switzerland

Switzerland is known for its stunning landscapes, including the Alps, and has four official languages: German, French, Italian, and Romansh. It is also famous for its high-quality chocolate, cheese, and being home to the world's longest and deepest railway tunnel, the Gotthard Base Tunnel.
